# Gustra Autoservisas – Design Ideas

## Idea 1 – "Baltic Craftsmanship" (Industrial Warmth)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Industrial Warmth  
**Core Principles:** Honest materials, warm neutrals, strong typographic hierarchy, functional clarity  
**Color Philosophy:** Warm off-white (#F8F4EF) backgrounds with deep charcoal (#1C1C1E) text, accents in amber/rust (#D97706) to evoke metal and heat — trustworthy, grounded  
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column hero with large left-aligned headline and right-side image; staggered card grid for services  
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rule dividers, bold condensed uppercase section labels, subtle grain texture overlay  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins, hover underline animations on nav, card lift on hover  
**Animation:** Entrance animations via framer-motion (slide-up + fade), staggered service cards  
**Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" for headings (bold, industrial), "Source Serif 4" for body (warm, readable)

## Idea 2 – "Clean Precision" (Modern Technical)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss Grid / Technical Modernism  
**Core Principles:** Precision layout, high contrast, clean white space, data-forward clarity  
**Color Philosophy:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) base, navy blue (#1E3A5F) as primary, electric blue (#2563EB) for CTAs — professional, trustworthy, automotive  
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width sticky top bar with phone CTA always visible; hero with diagonal split (image left, text right); tabbed service sections  
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal section dividers, numbered service steps, bold stat counters (20 years, etc.)  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Crisp hover states, animated counters on scroll, smooth tab transitions  
**Animation:** Counter animations, diagonal clip-path reveals, subtle parallax on hero  
**Typography System:** "DM Sans" for UI elements, "Playfair Display" for hero headline to add prestige
</text>
